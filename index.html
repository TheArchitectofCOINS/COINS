About C.O.I.N.S

C.O.I.N.S, LLC is building a decentralized future through innovation in digital currency and sports engagement. Our mission is to create powerful tools and experiences that connect communities and unlock new value in the Web3 ecosystem.

CoinToss

CoinToss is the flagship app of C.O.I.N.S, LLC — a sports prediction platform where bragging rights meet blockchain. Users make weekly picks, compete with friends, and earn rewards in SCOIN. Starting with college football, CoinToss is expanding to all major sports.

Standard Coin (SCOIN)

SCOIN is the utility token powering CoinToss and future C.O.I.N.S projects. With a controlled release supply and community-driven features, SCOIN is built to reward participation and expand utility over time.
