What is S-COIN Sports?

S-COIN Sports turns sports picks into a global game of skill. Make predictions, climb leaderboards, and earn recognition. The ecosystem runs on S-COIN for governance, staking, rewards, and perks — while S-COIN-FP powers free play so everyone can participate.

Future Vision

Today, S-COIN Sports is free-play only — anyone can join and enjoy the game without restrictions. But this is only the beginning. Once all legal requirements and licensing are in place, our roadmap includes the launch of real-money betting, bringing the same transparency and fairness that powers our free-play system. Free play today. Real stakes tomorrow.
