What is Game Day Sports?

Game Day Sports is a free-to-play sports prediction app. Make predictions, climb leaderboards, and earn recognition. The ecosystem runs on S-COIN for governance, staking, rewards, and perks — while S-COIN-FP powers free play so everyone can participate.

Future Vision

Today, Game Day Sports is free-play only — anyone can join and enjoy the game without restrictions. Once legal requirements and licensing are in place, our roadmap includes the launch of real-money betting. Free play today. Real stakes tomorrow.
