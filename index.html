What are GDT? Game Day Tokens (GDT) are free, in‑app tokens you earn by making correct picks, joining leaderboards, and engaging with the community. They represent bragging rights and future in‑app rewards — no purchase required.

About

What is Game Day Sports?

GDS is a sports picks experience built for fans. Make weekly predictions and earn Game Day Tokens (GDT) for bragging rights, leaderboards, and cosmetic rewards. The public demo is live while we finish the full multi-team build.

Game Day Tokens (GDT)

How free play works

There’s no purchase required for Game Day Tokens (GDT). You activate daily, make your picks, and climb the leaderboard. It’s designed to be fun and accessible while we expand features and data coverage.
