What We’re Building

Core Idea

S-COIN Sports lets fans make weekly picks, climb a transparent leaderboard, and earn in-ecosystem rewards. The mobile app is in development; you can preview work-in-progress on the Pre-Development page.

App status: Coming soon. Follow updates on X: @SCOINSPORTS.

Why Tokens?

Tokens align incentives: consistent, skillful picks should be visible and rewarded inside the ecosystem. The token family below separates free-play, utility, and stability roles so we can evolve features without mixing purposes.

The COINS Family

S-COIN-FP (Free-Play)

For demos, community games, and onboarding without paid balances. Great for testing new flows and formats.

S-COIN (Core Token)

The main utility token that powers features across the platform: entries, boosts, access, and ecosystem rewards.

S-COIN-S (Stable)

Stability-oriented counterpart used for treasuries, denominating rewards, and smooth settlement.

Presale Overview

Structure

  • Stage 2 & 3: TBD

We track totals in Supabase and show live progress on the Presale page. When a stage cap is reached, the next stage goes live on Smithii with updated pricing.

How Distribution Works

  1. Buy on Smithii during the active stage.
  2. We sync orders to our database to update totals.
  3. After presale ends, verified buyers are airdropped S-COIN to their wallet.
