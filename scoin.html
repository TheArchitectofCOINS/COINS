What We’re Building

Core Idea

S-COIN Sports lets fans make weekly picks, climb transparent leaderboards, and earn in-ecosystem rewards. The mobile app is in development; you can preview work-in-progress on the Pre-Development page.

App status: in development. Follow updates on X.

Why Tokens?

Tokens align incentives: consistent, skillful picks should be visible and rewarded. A dual-token design keeps things clean — free play and on-chain utility stay separate.

Two-Token Model

S-COIN On-chain

The flagship token for governance, staking, rewards, perks, and future expansion. Only S-COIN is sold in the presale.

  • Utility: access, boosts, governance votes
  • Incentives: referrals, promotions, partnerships
  • On-chain, tradable

S-COIN-FP Free Play

Free-play credits used for leaderboards, streaks, and bragging rights. Not on-chain, no KYC, and not sold.

  • Earned through gameplay
  • Used for cosmetics and scorekeeping
  • Keeps worldwide access open

Presale Overview

Structure

  • Total Presale: 100,000,000 S-COIN (1% of supply)
  • Tiers: 30M @ $0.05 • 30M @ $0.07 • 40M @ $0.10
  • Vesting: 25% at launch, then 25% each quarter (12 months total)

Reminder: S-COIN-FP is free-play only and is not part of the sale.

Use of Funds

  • Treasury & liquidity
  • Rewards & incentives for growth
  • Development, audits, and ecosystem expansion

FAQ (Short)

Is S-COIN the only token I can buy?

Yes. The presale sells S-COIN only. S-COIN-FP is free-play credit and isn’t sold.

What wallets are supported?

Solana wallets (e.g., Phantom). More details will be added before launch.

How are allocations handled?

Presale is capped by tier. Tokens vest 25% at launch, then 25% per quarter.
